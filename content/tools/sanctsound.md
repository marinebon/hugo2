SanctSound presents results from the Sanctuary Soundscape Monitoring Project, a partnership between NOAA and the U.S. Navy to understand underwater sound within the National Marine Sanctuary System. Illustrated panels walk through what was measured, where and when, and what it means for marine life and people.
