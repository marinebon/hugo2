The MBON is pleased to announce that Dr. Frank Muller-Karger, MBON co-chair, was recognized by The Oceanography Society (TOS) 2024 Honors as a Fellow for his exceptional achievement and contributions to ocean sciences.

The citation recognizes him for extraordinary endowment and leadership in furthering our understanding of Earth and, in particular, the ocean, through the use of remote sensing and field work.

Dr. Muller-Karger's achievements will be celebrated during The Oceanography Society's Honors Breakfast taking place on February 21, 2024, during the Ocean Sciences Meeting.

You can learn more about the 2024 TOS Honors recipients [here](https://tos.org/tos-honors-2024#fellows).
