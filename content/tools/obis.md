The Ocean Biodiversity Information System (OBIS) is the world's largest open-access repository of marine biodiversity data, hosted by the IOC-UNESCO International Oceanographic Data and Information Exchange (IODE). It aggregates species occurrence records from thousands of datasets — including the MBON network — standardized to the Darwin Core schema with extended measurement-or-fact (eMoF) extensions and OBIS quality control.

Most MBON datasets are published to OBIS, which is why the MBON Data catalog treats the OBIS record as canonical when a dataset is mirrored across portals. Use the searchable catalog and the interactive [OBIS Mapper](https://mapper.obis.org/) to explore occurrences by taxon, region, dataset, or time, then download records through the catalog or the OBIS API.
