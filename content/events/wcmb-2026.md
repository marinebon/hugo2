The **7th World Conference on Marine Biodiversity (WCMB 2026)** — themed *"The marine
biodiversity insights we need, for the ocean we want"* — is organized by the Flanders
Marine Institute (VLIZ) and endorsed as a UN Ocean Decade event. Taking place a little
over halfway through the Decade, it is an opportunity to take stock of ongoing actions
and evaluate what remains to be tackled.

MBON convenes and contributes to sessions on measuring the current state of marine
biodiversity, leveraging the international collaborations of the Marine Biodiversity
Observation Network of GEO BON and the Marine Life 2030 programme.
