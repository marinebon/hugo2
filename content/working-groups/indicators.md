The Indicators working group bridges raw observational data and decision-relevant reporting. Working group members develop, validate, and publish Essential Biodiversity Variables (EBVs) and Essential Ocean Variables (EOVs) derived from MBON node data streams.

Products include status-and-trends dashboards, indicator time series submitted to GEO BON, and synthesis papers linking biodiversity indicators to ecosystem services and climate-change impacts.
