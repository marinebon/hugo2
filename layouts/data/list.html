The bigger picture

FAIR by design

MBON observations are standardized to Darwin Core and published to open repositories so they are Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable. The Ocean Biodiversity Information System (OBIS) is the primary home for biological occurrence data; GBIF aggregates the broader OBIS network; the Environmental Data Initiative (EDI) archives long-term ecological packages; and ERDDAP servers stream gridded and sensor data.
