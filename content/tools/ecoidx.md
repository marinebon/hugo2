ecoidx provides reusable R functions for producing marine ecological indicator time series and visualizations following NOAA Integrated Ecosystem Assessment (IEA) standards, used in California Current ecosystem status reporting. It is one of the indicator libraries highlighted in the MBON ocean-indicators software portfolio.
